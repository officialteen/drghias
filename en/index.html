About Dr. Ghias

26 Years of Excellence in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia

Dr. Ghias Attar is a board-certified specialist in oral surgery, dental implants, and cosmetic prosthetics practicing in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia for over 26 years. Registered with the Saudi Commission for Health Specialties (SCHS), he combines surgical precision with a refined aesthetic sensibility in every procedure — from a single implant to a full mouth restoration.

Dental Implants in Jeddah

Dr. Ghias is among Jeddah's most experienced dental implant surgeons. He works with premium titanium and zirconia implants from leading global brands, offering same-day immediate implant placement in qualifying cases. Treatment planning includes precise 3D imaging and digital diagnostics to ensure optimal outcomes. Services extend to bone grafting, sinus lift procedures, and structured post-op follow-up. All implants come with a warranty. With an implant success rate exceeding 98%, patients travel from across Saudi Arabia and the wider region specifically for his expertise.

Hollywood Smile & Cosmetic Dentistry

Dr. Ghias specializes in complete smile makeovers using porcelain veneers and zirconia crowns crafted with CAD/CAM technology. Every smile design begins with digital imaging and a proposed smile simulation before any treatment starts — so patients see the expected result in advance. Professional teeth whitening is also available for cases that require color improvement without reshaping. Whether you seek a subtle enhancement or a full smile makeover, the clinic delivers natural-looking, long-lasting results tailored to your face.

Oral Surgery & Root Canal Therapy

With extensive experience in complex surgical extractions — including impacted wisdom teeth and difficult root morphologies — Dr. Ghias provides comfortable, precise oral surgery. Root canal therapy is performed with rotary endodontic instruments for painless, efficient treatment. Gum contouring and black gum depigmentation services address both functional and aesthetic concerns, transforming gummy smiles and smoker's gum discoloration into healthy, confident smiles.
